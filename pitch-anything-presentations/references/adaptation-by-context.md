# Adaptation by context

## Internal corporate presentation
Emphasize:
- risk reduction
- strategic alignment
- feasibility
- rollout control
- operational clarity
- cost of inaction

## Sales presentation
Emphasize:
- pain
- emotional relief
- differentiated mechanism
- proof
- urgency
- easy next step

## Interview presentation
Emphasize:
- narrative credibility
- calm authority
- impact
- selectivity
- fit
- measurable outcomes

## Product pitch
Emphasize:
- user pain
- hidden friction
- transformed workflow
- category framing
- proof of adoption
- decision path

## Fundraising or proposal
Emphasize:
- market shift
- strategic inevitability
- leverage
- asymmetry
- proof
- why this team / why this approach

## Rule
Tune the deck to the decision environment, not just the topic.
